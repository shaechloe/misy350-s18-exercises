Welcome to DogBase!

DogBase is a web application that allows users to add, update and delete groups and breeds of dogs in a database. It may sound strange that dogs are apart of groups. You may think, dogs are canines and that is that. However, in dog shows there is need to be able to break dogs in to groups to able to judge them accuratly and fairly. There are 7 groups that dogs can be put into: Terrier, Toy, Working, Sporting, Non-Sporting, Hound, and Herding. Dog breeds are assigned to one of seven groups representing characteristics and functions the breeds were originally bred for.

Dog Groups have changed in the past and can change in the future and DogBase allows for these changes. DogBase lets you add, delete and change groups and, with referral integrity, the corisponding breeds change with it. DogBase also alows for users to add new dog breeds and change breeds.

I hope you enjoy DogBase!
